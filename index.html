About

Hello there! Welcome to Skillit. Unfortunately, we're still in development so please be patient with us!

Skillit helps students and professionals discover new courses so that they can improve themselves. Think of it as the Pandora for courses. All we need is for your to connect your LinkedIn profile and our algorithm will take care of the rest.

Our mission is simple. It's scary preparing yourself in the job world. Sometimes you need a hand, and that's why we're here. We want to provide you with the resources so you can succeed, whatever that may be and wherever you may be in your career trajectory.
